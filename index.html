About

I am an M.S. student at Tsinghua University (SIGS), affiliated with the SSR Group under the supervision of Prof. Wenbo Ding. I received my B.S. in Theoretical and Applied Mechanics from Tsinghua University, where I was selected for the Tsinghua Xuetang Talents Program (Qian Xuesen Class / 钱学森力学班). My research focuses on visuotactile sensing, robotic simulation (Isaac Sim / FEM), Sim2Real transfer, and precision manipulation. I aim to bridge the gap between high-fidelity tactile simulation and real-world robotic deployment.
